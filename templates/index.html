What is this?

UK nail bars pay business rates based on their property's rateable value — a government estimate of how much the property could be rented for. When that rateable value is high relative to turnover, the nail bar is screwed.

Our Screwed Score compares rateable value against estimated turnover. A score over 110 means the nail bar is paying over the odds in rates.

✅ Under 95 — Doing alright
🔵 95–109 — A bit screwed
🟡 110–124 — Pretty screwed
🔴 125+ — Royally screwed
